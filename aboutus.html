About Us

Who we are

Welcome to NMT Motor Spares, your one-stop-shop for all your automotive needs. We are a leading supplier of quality new, used, and reconditioned auto parts, providing our customers with the best value for their money. Our extensive range of auto parts makes us a go-to destination for vehicle owners and repair shops alike.

Our Mission

Our mission at NMT Motor Spares is to be the go-to destination for all your automotive needs. We are committed to providing our customers with high-quality auto parts at affordable prices, and exceptional customer service. We strive to build long-lasting relationships with our customers and to be a reliable partner in their vehicle maintenance and repair needs. Our goal is to continue to expand our inventory and stay up-to-date with the latest trends in the automotive industry, so that we can provide our customers with the best products and services possible.

Why choose us

At NMT Motor Spares, we believe in providing exceptional customer service and building strong relationships with our customers. We have a team of highly skilled professionals with a deep understanding of the automotive industry, and we always go the extra mile to offer expert advice and guidance on all your auto parts needs. We stock only the best parts sourced from reputable manufacturers and suppliers, ensuring that all our parts meet the highest standards of quality and reliability. Our inventory is updated regularly to ensure that we have the latest and most in-demand parts in stock. We cater to both trade customers and DIY enthusiasts, so no matter what your needs are, we have the knowledge and experience to help you find the right parts for your vehicle. We offer a range of delivery options to ensure your parts arrive on time and in perfect condition.

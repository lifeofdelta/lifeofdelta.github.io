Who we are

NMT Motor Spares is a 2010-founded auto sales and parts business that has been delivering high-quality services and goods to customers for over 12 years. Our extensive range of new, used, and reconditioned auto parts make us a one-stop-shop for vehicle owners and repair shops alike. We take pride in being a leading supplier of quality auto parts at affordable prices, providing our customers with the best value for their money.

Our Mission

Our mission at NMT Motor Spares is to provide our customers with the best quality auto parts at affordable prices, backed by exceptional customer service. We strive to build strong relationships with our customers and to be the go-to destination for all their automotive needs. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do.

Why choose us

At NMT Motor Spares, we believe in providing our customers with the best value for their money. We stock only the best quality auto parts, sourced from reputable manufacturers and suppliers, and rigorously tested to ensure they meet the highest standards of quality and reliability. We offer a wide range of auto parts at affordable prices, catering to both trade customers and DIY enthusiasts. With our knowledgeable and experienced team, you can be assured of expert advice and guidance on all your auto parts needs.

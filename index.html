Who we are

NMT Motor Spares is a 2010-founded auto sales and parts business that has been delivering high-quality services and goods to customers for over 12 years. Specifically specializing in mechanical repairs, NMT Motor Group was a car service facility. However, NMT's agility has allowed it to quickly adapt to the market changes and turn into a retailer of high-quality, reasonably priced automobiles and auto parts.

Our Mission

Our goal is to be the best location for all vehicle-related services (including the buying and selling of vehicles as well as the sales of car parts and accessories) and to provide customers with products that are trustworthy, affordable, and safe while also providing the best customer service. We would love to grow our business into a major one with branches throughout Southern Africa and beyond.

Why choose us

Most cars and car parts are very expensive, we provide alternatives that are reasonable. Genuine parts and affordable vehicles. We offer lower prices than everyone else.
